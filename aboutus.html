About Us

We are a concept restaurant co-owned by Shun Chen, ZongShu Zhu and Wen Wang. Shun Chen is the owner of Fuji Yama, a popular Japanese restaurant on East Beltline in Grand Rapids. Lucky Noodle serves ramen, sushi, maki rolls, maki burritos, sashimi and seaweed salad made on-site, as well as bubble tea and Japanese soda. We also have catering services available for parties and gatherings.

MEET THE TEAM

ZongShu Zhu, the head sushi chef, moved from New York City start Lucky Noodle. He enjoys making ornate decorated sushi displays and creating unique sushi appetizers.

Johnny Lin, the head kitchen chef has worked for some really cool ramen restaurant in New York City where he learned from a real Japanese guy. He moved to Grand Rapids to create his own menu from what he's learned.

Shun Chen is the owner of multiple restaurants in West Michigan. Fujiyama, Sakura, and Sushi Maki you may know. He hopes to bring a different type of menu to Grand Rapids with Lucky Noodle.
