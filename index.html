We are a concept restaurant co-owned by Shun Chen, ZongShu Zhu and Wen Wang. Shun Chen is the owner of Fuji Yama, a popular Japanese restaurant on East Beltline in Grand Rapids. Lucky Noodle serves ramen, sushi, maki rolls, maki burritos, sashimi and seaweed salad made on-site, as well as bubble tea and Japanese soda. We also have catering services available for parties and gatherings.
